BMI Based Suggestion

Body mass index (BMI) is a value derived from the weight and height of a person. The BMI is defined as the body mass divided by the square of the body height,and is expressed in units of kg/m2, resulting from mass in kilograms and height in metres

Normal Body mass index range : 18.5 to 24.9
